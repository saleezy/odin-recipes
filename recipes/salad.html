Summer Salad

Description

If you grew up with ambrosia salads at your potlucks and holiday dinners, you most likely have your family's staple recipe in mind. But, if you're looking to celebrate spring with a throwback Southern dessert this year, we think our classic recipe really takes the cake. This Southern fruit salad is a classic for very good reason: Full of fruits, nuts and coconut all tossed in a sweet and creamy combo, it's our ideal kind of salad.

Ingredients

  • cool whip
  • sour cream
  • mini marshmallows
  • mandarin oranges
  • fresh pineapple
  • shredded coconut
  • cherries
  • toasted pecans

Preparation

  1. Mix Cool whip and other dry ingredients
  2. Chop up veggies
  3. Mix in to dressing
  4. Cover and refrigerate
